Who We Are

FreeWill Finance is an Australian finance broking firm specialising in investment-focused & owner occupier lending solutions. We help investors & owner occupiers structure finance correctly from day one — with a long-term view.

Our Approach

We don’t believe in one-size-fits-all finance. Every recommendation is tailored to your investment strategy, ownership structure, and future goals — not just today’s interest rate.

We work with owner occupiers, first-time investors, experienced portfolio builders, and sophisticated clients investing through trusts, SMSFs and companies across Australia.
